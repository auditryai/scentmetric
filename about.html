About ScentMetric

Transparent, data-backed fragrance recommendations

A $300 cologne contains $10-20 of ingredients. The rest is marketing and brand tax. Meanwhile, Reddit and fragrance forums have been identifying near-identical alternatives for years.

ScentMetric structures that community knowledge into transparent, scored rankings. Every dupe is rated across Similarity, Performance, Projection, and Longevity — with every score traceable to its source.

Our Scoring

Four metrics, weighted by importance. Similarity (40%), Performance (20%), Projection (20%), Longevity (20%).
